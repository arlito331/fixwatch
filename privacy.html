FixWatch Privacy Policy

PowerFix Inc. · Last updated July 22, 2026

FixWatch is an internal field tool used by PowerFix crews to document road repairs made with PowerPatch: where a repair was installed, when, and how it holds up over time in photos.

What the app collects

  • Location — only when you tap "Use my current location" while logging a repair, to place the repair's pin on the map. The app does not track your location in the background.
  • Photos — only the photos you choose to attach to a repair's timeline, from your camera or photo library.
  • Repair details you type — labels, dates, notes, city and country of the repair.

What the app does not do

  • No accounts, no sign-up, and no collection of names, emails, or contacts.
  • No advertising, no analytics trackers, no sale or sharing of data with third parties.
  • No background location tracking.

Where data is stored

Repair records and photos are stored in a PowerFix-controlled repository on GitHub (GitHub, Inc.), which serves as the team's shared database. The team access code you enter is stored only on your device.

Your choices

Location and photo access are optional and requested only when you use those features; you can decline in iOS Settings at any time. To request removal of a photo or record, contact us and we will delete it from the repository.
